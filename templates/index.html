A Machine Learning Approach to Predicting Customer Churn

The Telecommunication Customer Churn Prediction System forecasts customer departure likelihoods for service providers. It aggregates and processes data from diverse sources, including customer interactions, usage patterns, and billing details. After refining the data, machine learning techniques are applied to identify key churn factors like service satisfaction and interaction quality. Techniques such as Logistic Regression and Random Forest are utilized to develop predictive models. The best-performing model is then implemented for practical use. Telecom companies can thus proactively offer personalized services and promotions to retain at-risk customers. However, the system faces challenges like ensuring data privacy, adapting to dynamic market conditions, and integrating with existing infrastructures. Overall, this system is essential for telecoms aiming to improve customer retention and stay competitive.
